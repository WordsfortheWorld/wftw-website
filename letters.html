Send a Letter!


This page is regularly updated with guidelines for the specific recipients to whom we are currently writing. Please follow them closely and mail all letters to this address:

PO BOX ADDRESS


*If you would like to receive confirmation once your letter has been received by Words for the World, please include a sticky note inside your envelope with your name, email, and the date the letter was sent.

Currently writing to: Rainbow Community Center
LGBTQIA+ Support Community

For all letters:

  1. Do not include personal identifying information. Pseudonyms are welcome.
  2. Do not include any mention of LGBTQIA+. Please keep in mind that we do not want to risk outing anyone.
  3. Please refrain from asking questions. This would encourage a reply letter which we are currently unable to support.
  4. Do not use glitter or loose bits. Pen is encouraged over pencil.
  5. Be respectful.


OPTION ONE: CHECK-IN LETTERS distributed with the Kind Hearts Food Drive

  • Please include some variety of “Is there anything Rainbow can assist with? If so, please contact cristina@rainbowcc.org.”
  • Feel free to talk about happenings in your life as if to a friend.


OPTION TWO: Thank you letters to Rainbow Community Center volunteers

  • Example sentences:
    • Thank you for _____ (volunteering/helping/contributing) to/for the Rainbow Community Center.
    • Rainbow is able to exist thanks to volunteers like you.
    • Thank you on behalf of the Rainbow Community Center for sparing your valuable time to volunteer and support us in our cause.


OPTION THREE: Uplifting cards to be distributed at a variety of events

  • A card can have a quick uplifting message like “You're amazing!”
